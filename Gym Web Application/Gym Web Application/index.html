About Us

Welcome to SG FitnessClub, where every drop of sweat tells a story of dedication and triumph. Our journey began in 2010, driven by a passionate vision to create a fitness sanctuary that transcends the ordinary.

Founding Principles

  • Founded by fitness enthusiasts Shreyash Gandhi, SG FitnessClub was built on the principles of inclusivity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to our members' well-being. We envisioned a space where individuals of all fitness levels, backgrounds, and ages could come together to embark on a transformative fitness journey.

    • Building a Community

  • Beyond the barbells and treadmills, SG FitnessClub is a place where friendships are forged, and successes are celebrated. Our thriving community shares not just workouts but also a common goal — to live healthier, stronger lives.

    • Milestones and Achievements

  • As we reflect on our journey, we take pride in the countless success stories and transformations that have unfolded within our walls. Whether it's achieving a personal best, overcoming obstacles, or finding newfound confidence, each milestone is a testament to the spirit of SG FitnessClub.
